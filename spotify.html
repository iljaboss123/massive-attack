PCA and Clustering

PC1 (The Intensity Axis): Usually captures the biggest difference in the data. For Massive Attack, this often separates the "Heavy/Aggressive" tracks (high energy, high loudness) from the "Atmospheric/Trip-Hop" tracks (high acousticness). PC2 (The Complexity/Mood Axis): Often captures the second-largest pattern, such as the difference between "Electronic/Instrumental" tracks and "Vocal/Poppy" tracks (Valence and Speechiness).
