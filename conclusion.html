Conclusion

In this project I learned a lot about my favorite musical collective. Most importantly, I never realized how simple the harmony on most of their songs is, as they have incredibly polished production overall it works so well i just never noticed it.
I also held the expectations that the Spotify API features would be these all powerful song explaining features, but found out they miss a lot of what actually defines a sound, and just don't work so well. So I mostly learned the power of audio visualisations to compare and explain tracks, and show how rhythm and texture shape a track.

Contributions

Insights like these could be very useful for a producer, to prove that a simple but strong melody can be the base of a very good track, as good production all round does not always need very complex harmony. And also the combination of listening to a song while having visualizations of it in front of you will teach you a lot about the song, and gives you insights you can take into your own production.
